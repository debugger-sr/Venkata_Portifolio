My Story

My journey into data began with a fascination for uncovering patterns. I started in analytics, driven by the desire to answer complex business questions. However, I quickly realized that the quality of any insight is fundamentally limited by the quality of the underlying data infrastructure.

This realization pushed me into data engineering, where I learned to build the systems that ensure data is reliable, timely, and accessible. Today, I operate at the intersection of these two worlds. I don't just analyze data; I build the foundations it rests upon. This dual perspective allows me to create truly end-to-end data solutions that are both technically sound and strategically valuable.
